Web Developer

Location: Remote

Job Type: Full-time

Responsibilities

  • Develop responsive and user-friendly websites and web applications using modern technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and various web frameworks.
  • Collaborate with designers, project managers, and other developers to deliver high-quality solutions that meet client requirements and project deadlines.
  • Perform code reviews, debugging, and troubleshooting to ensure the stability and performance of web projects.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and best practices to continuously improve skills and contribute innovative ideas to projects.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
  • Proven experience in web development with proficiency in front-end and/or back-end technologies.
  • Strong understanding of web development principles, including responsive design, cross-browser compatibility, and accessibility standards.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.
